About us

PetsHelp is a project whose idea is to help homeless animals, treat them, rehabilitate them, and place them in a family. Our goal is for every animal to have a loving owner. We strive for the shelter to be a temporary shelter for animals, and not their permanent home.

You can take an animal from a shelter, or adopt an animal if you found it on the street.
